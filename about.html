Who We Are

Building Intelligent AI Solutions For Modern Businesses

HiorbAI is a frontend AI company portfolio project created to demonstrate modern web development techniques, responsive user interfaces, and interactive AI-inspired tools. The website showcases how Artificial Intelligence solutions can be presented through a clean and engaging digital experience.

The project includes practical features such as an AI Project Cost Calculator, AI Readiness Assessment, Analytics Dashboard, interactive charts, responsive layouts, and LocalStorage integration, making it suitable for academic presentations and portfolio demonstrations.
